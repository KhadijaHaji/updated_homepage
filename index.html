Welcome to khadija's personal homepage!

Hi, my name is Khadija and I've made this website to tell you all about my journey into Tech!

I have joined an amazing course provided by Code Your Future. I'm currently in the middle of completing the digital course they have a available and so far I've really been enjoying it!

I have decided to embark this journey to prove to myself that despite having thought that I was a non "technical" person, I can challenge myself and achieve great things.

I hope that after completing the digital course I can then move on to the Software Development Course.

I have successfully applied to a part time job so I can provide for myself whilst I focus on the course.

I hope that I can look back at my journey fondly in the future!
